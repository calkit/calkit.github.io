Calkit

A lightweight framework for reproducible research

Put everything in one place: Code, data, environments, figures, publications, and more, to maximize efficiency and impact.

View the docsLogin to calkit.io


flash_on

Increase productivity

When your workflows are reproducible, you will avoid getting lost and make fewer mistakes. Iterations on artifacts like figures and publications become much faster.

group

Maximize impact

By putting all of your research inputs and outputs into a single project repository with a single pipeline, others will be able to reproduce your work and adapt it to create new knowledge, use it to develop new technology, etc.

settings

Open source

Calkit is open source and built upon other open source technology such as Git, Docker, DVC, and more.

Increase productivity

When your project is single-button reproducible, you will avoid getting lost and make fewer mistakes. Iterations on artifacts like figures and publications become much faster.

Maximize impact

By putting all of your research inputs and outputs into one project with a single pipeline, others will be able to reproduce your work, make predictions with it, and adapt it to create new knowledge, use it to develop new technology, etc.

Lock in without lock-in

Calkit is open source and built upon other open source technology such as Git, Docker, DVC, and more. Use whatever languages you like: Python, R, MATLAB, LaTeX, or all of the above!
